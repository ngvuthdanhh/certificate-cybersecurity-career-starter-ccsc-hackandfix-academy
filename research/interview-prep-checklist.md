# Extra 1 — Cybersecurity Interview Preparation Checklist

## 1. Purpose
Prepare for cybersecurity interviews with focus on analytical credibility, incident reasoning, hypothesis clarity, and sector-aware risk thinking.

---

## 2. Checklist
[ ] Understand attacker motives and TTP mapping
[ ] Practice 3 incident hypotheses per scenario
[ ] Correlate signals under time pressure
[ ] Scope organizational impact in 90 seconds
[ ] Produce 150-word incident summary clearly
[ ] Explain confidence rating logically
[ ] Present portfolio structure professionally
[ ] Avoid over-explaining security tools
[ ] Provide defensive recommendations, not exploits
